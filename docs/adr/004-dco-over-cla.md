# ADR-004: DCO over CLA

**Status:** Accepted

## Decision

Contributions require Developer Certificate of Origin sign-off (`git commit -s`), not a Contributor License Agreement.

## Rationale

A CLA is friction and asymmetry (contributors grant rights to a single entity) that an Apache-2.0 community project doesn't need. DCO keeps provenance auditable per-commit with no paperwork and no gatekeeper.
